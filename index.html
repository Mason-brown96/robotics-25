Space Robotics

Welcome

This subject presents a broad overview of the technologies associated with robotics for space environments and related applications of modern robotic systems. Major topics covered are sensing, mapping, navigation, path planning, and the coordination of robots in planetary and microgravity settings. The subject consists of classes on robot fundamentals and case studies on practical robot systems. Class material is illustrated through laboratory problem sets and a group assessment. The subject aims to give students the essential skills to develop robotic systems for particular space applications and related broader contexts.
